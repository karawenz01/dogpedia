Breed Finder

Check the characteristics you would like your dog to have and we will show you dog breeds that suit your needs!

Apartment-friendly?

You should have an answer to the following questions. Will he bark a lot and disturb your neighbors? Can he get enough exercise with no backyard? Is his coat high maintenance? Is he too large? Will he be friendly toward other people and pets?

High Energy level?

If you’re a dedicated couch potato, you need a dog who loves to be lazy. But if you’re looking for a jogging buddy, a playmate, or you lead a very active lifestyle — or have kids who can’t stop moving! — a pup with high energy may be a perfect fit.

Amount of shedding?

Different dog breeds shed in different amounts: Some shed seasonally, while others shed year-round. For dog’s who shed often, it is important to brush them — sometimes weekly or sometimes daily during periods of heavy shedding.
